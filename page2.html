After a quick rest, I remember the non-trademarked soda I have in my inventory, so I equip that into my hand. The cap is stuck and I’m trying my best to get it open. All of a sudden the cap loosens and flies off the bottle. To my misfortune, the sharp bottle cap pierces my balloon dog. I watch my method of transportation deflate. I quickly begin to panic, I can see the depth of what I expected to be a stream be a full-on bottomless body of water. I have no idea how to swim in this game because of course, swimming mechanics in every game is so different and difficult.
