Until the Code Runs Out...

Woah. I wake up on the ground. I have no clue where I am, or what’s happened in the last few hours. I’m in a strange town. I get myself up and notice something is off.

I’m feeling weird. I look down at my legs, they’re pixels. I look at my hands, pixels. I run my nub through my hair and it goes right through. I’m complete code. Then I realize what’s happened. I’m inside my game.
